Equinox Nexus 🌍

Agentic Financial Digital Twin for Global Workforce Mobility

Equinox Nexus is an AI-powered platform that creates a Financial Digital Twin of an individual or workforce and simulates future financial outcomes across countries before relocation, hiring, or expansion decisions are made.

Instead of providing static recommendations, Equinox Nexus models future scenarios, predicts financial risks, evaluates compliance requirements, and forecasts workforce costs using autonomous AI agents.
